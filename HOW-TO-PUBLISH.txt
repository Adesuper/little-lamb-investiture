HOW TO GET A SHAREABLE LINK (open from any phone or computer)
=============================================================

This folder ("investiture-program") is the whole investiture program — the
6 slides, all photos, the music videos, and the "Oh What a Miracle" video —
bundled together. Nothing else is needed.

EASIEST WAY — Netlify Drop (free, no account needed, ~2 minutes)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open your web browser and go to:   https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Open this "investiture-program" folder in your file explorer.
3. Drag the WHOLE folder onto the Netlify Drop page ("Drag and drop your site").
4. Wait for the upload (it's ~168 MB, so give it a minute on good wifi).
5. Netlify shows you a link like  https://random-name-12345.netlify.app
   -> That is your shareable link. Open it on any phone or computer.

To make the link nicer or update it later:
- Click "Claim this site" / sign up free with Google — keeps the link permanent.
- In Site settings you can rename it, e.g.  little-lamb-investiture.netlify.app
- To update after a change: drag the folder onto the same site's "Deploys" page.

USING THE PROGRAM
-----------------
- It opens on the "Run of Show" (Step 1). Use Next / Prev or the sidebar.
- Press F (or "Show Audience") for big full-screen mode for the projector.
- Step 2 plays the year slideshow; Step 4 the music videos; Step 5 the song video.

Tip: on the projector, click into the page once first so the browser allows
the video/music to play with sound.
